Master blaster Sachin Tendulkar married to beautiful Anjali On 24 May 1995 at the age of 22. 
Anjali, is the daughter of Gujarati industrialist Anand Mehta and British social worker Annabel Mehta.
She is six years elder than Sachin. Couple have two children, Sara and Arjun. 
Arjun is a left handed batsman, has recently been included in under-14 probables list of Mumbai Cricket Association for off-season training camp.
