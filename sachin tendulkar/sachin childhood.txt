Sachin Tendulkar, needs no introduction to the fans of cricket. 
He was born on April 24, 1973 at Nirmal Nursing Home, Dadar, Mumbai, India.
He is the son of reputed Marathi novelist Mr. Ramesh Tendulkar and his mother Rajni worked in the insurance industry.
His father named him Sachin after his favourite music director, Sachin Dev Burman.
Sachin has three elder siblings, two half-brothers Nitin and Ajit, and a half-sister Savita.
He was considered to be a tormentor in his school life as he often took up fights with new children in his school.
